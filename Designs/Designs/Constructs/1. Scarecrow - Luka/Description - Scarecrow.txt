

Name: Scarecrow
Power: 1/10

Description:
-The first construct Johanna creates, it's made out of wood, sticks, rope, a skull head, a straw hat and some mechanical parts INSIDE the skull head.
-The mechanical parts allow it to shoot small thunder bolts and make its eyes glow red for a short moment.

Size: 
The scarecrow is 1,6 meters tall.
